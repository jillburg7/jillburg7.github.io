How to Play

Objective: Guess the hidden code peg combination in 10 guesses or less.
To start a new game, simply click the "New" button.
To reveal code after a game is started, click "Reveal" button.
Guess Peg Controls:
  • To add - Click on a color in the color peg panel on the right.
  • To remove - Click on the guess peg in the guess panel on the bottom.
  • To submit guess, click the "Check" button.
Feedback Peg Key:
  • Black peg - Correct color in the correct position.
  • White peg - Correct color in the wrong position.
  • Gray peg - Empty peg; incorrect color.
The order in which the feedback pegs are displayed does not have any relation to the order & color of the guess pegs.
